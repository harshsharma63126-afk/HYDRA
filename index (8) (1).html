Questions

Frequently Asked.

What documents do I need for self-drive?

A valid driving licence, Aadhaar card, and a refundable security deposit. That's it — we handle the rest.

Do you deliver the car to my location?

Yes. Free home delivery anywhere in Jaipur. Outstation delivery available on request.

Are the cars insured?

Every vehicle in our fleet carries comprehensive insurance and 24×7 roadside assistance.

Can I take the car outstation?

Absolutely. We offer outstation packages to Udaipur, Jaisalmer, Delhi, Pushkar and more.

What's included in wedding packages?

Decorated luxury car, uniformed driver, fuel for local use, and on-site coordination. Custom packages available.

How do I cancel or modify a booking?

Just WhatsApp us at least 6 hours before pickup for full flexibility.
